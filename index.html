The Nick Warren Story: Well to be honest, I'm actually not that good at talking about myself. I am a Geologist by education where I graduated from the University of Colorado with my bachelors in Geology (Cum Laude) and a minor in Business Administration.

Being a Geologist is great but my passion lies more in hunting for rocks and minerals. The thrill of the "hunt" is incredibly exhilirating. To find a fossil, for instance, that has been underground for millions of years and hasn't seen the light of day in that same amount of time, is mind boggling. To be the first human to find it is where words can not describe the feeling.

In addition to hunting for rocks and minerals, I am also an avid scuba diver. I currently have been on 12 dives in Jamaica and the Bahamas. My wife, who became certified long before me, has dove in the Great Barrior Reef in addition to dives we have been on together. >
